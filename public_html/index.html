About Us

Michelle and Jerry Hicks started Rainbow Snow Santa Fe for their three daughters as a summer hobby. Rainbow Snow has been around for 35 plus years and as a young girl Michelle knew one day she would own it! When Michelle and Jerry took the business over they completely rejuvenated the units and the product itself. From a new interior along with painting of the exterior to getting new machines and changing the flavors along with adding ice cream and many other toppings! Along the way Rainbow Snow has been very successful for the 5 years they have owned it and it has now become apart of their family. They now have 2 units along with a mobile van for special events! Rainbow Snow continues to grow and they will potentially get a permanent location. We want to be the Shaved Ice that everyone talks about, that people are excited to come to Santa Fe for.
